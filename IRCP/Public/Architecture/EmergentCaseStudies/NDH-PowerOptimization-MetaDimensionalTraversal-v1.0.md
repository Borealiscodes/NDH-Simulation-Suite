# ⭐ **2. Background: Why Dimensionality Matters**

### 2.1 3D Systems  
3D latent spaces (e.g., spatial + channel tensors):

- Lower FLOPs  
- Lower memory bandwidth  
- Lower GPU interconnect load  
- Lower cooling demand  
- Lower inference cost  

### 2.2 50D Systems  
50D latent spaces:

- Higher FLOPs  
- Higher memory traffic  
- Higher interconnect pressure  
- Higher cooling demand  
- Higher inference cost  
- Higher carbon footprint  

### 2.3 Why NDH’s Projection Systems Matter  
NDH includes **projection operators** that map:

\[
\mathcal{M}_{50D} \rightarrow \mathcal{M}_{3D}
\]

for tasks that **do not require** full manifold richness.

This is the core energy‑saving mechanism.
