---

## **1. Purpose**
This precursor layer provides **meta‑coherent awareness** of unsafe‑concept behavior across all manifolds and simulation environments.  
It does **not** contain unsafe concepts, route them, or execute quarantine logic.  
It only:

- observes  
- maps  
- detects drift  
- identifies fragmentation  
- maintains coherence  
- informs the roadmap for building the registry  

This layer is **pure awareness**, not execution.

## **2. Meta Coherence Operators**

### **2.1 Warden (Boundary Awareness Operator)**  
Warden maintains awareness of **where** unsafe concepts exist across the system.

Functions:
- Boundary mapping across Manifold 0, Simulation Suite, IRCP  
- Awareness of all quarantine zones  
- Detection of duplicate or orphaned unsafe archives  
- Awareness of containment scope (root vs mirror vs pointer)  
- Awareness of contamination risk zones  

Warden does **not** enforce boundaries—only perceives them.

Guided Link: **Warden Operator**

---

### **2.2 Monitoring (Behavior Awareness Operator)**  
Monitoring maintains awareness of **how** unsafe concepts are referenced.

Functions:
- Observes reference patterns (direct, indirect, global)  
- Detects contamination risk in awareness layers  
- Tracks drift between Manifold 0 and Simulation Suite  
- Identifies when new unsafe archives are created instead of referencing canonical ones  
- Maintains coherence of routing behavior  

Monitoring does **not** correct behavior—only perceives it.

Guided Link: **Monitoring Operator**
