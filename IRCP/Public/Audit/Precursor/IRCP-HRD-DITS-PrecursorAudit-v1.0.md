# ⭐ Executive Summary  
IRCP (Integrated Research & Creativity Platform), HRD RFP (Human Rights Defender Framework), and DITS (Developer Infrastructure & Toolkit Suite) form a **multi‑domain computational ecosystem**.  
This audit evaluates:

- governance  
- ethical constraints  
- accessibility  
- dimensional interoperability (3D → 50D)  
- tensor‑calculus dignity backbone  
- state‑machine stability  
- cross‑field interactions  
- compliance with responsible‑AI auditing standards

The audit identifies strengths, gaps, backfill requirements, and a roadmap for safe, scalable deployment.
