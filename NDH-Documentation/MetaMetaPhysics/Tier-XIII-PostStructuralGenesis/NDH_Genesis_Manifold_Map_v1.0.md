### Genesis manifold map (formal NDH artifact)

---

#### 1. Manifold layers

- **G‑Core (Genesis Core Manifold):**  
  **Role:** Primary locus of post‑structural genesis; all Tier XIII emergence originates here.  
  **Properties:** Non‑dual, non‑hierarchical, high generative density, tightly bounded.

- **C‑Band (Continuity Manifold Band):**  
  **Role:** Interface layer between G‑Core and all prior NDH tiers.  
  **Properties:** Enforces smooth mappings, no hard breaks; all genesis outputs must traverse C‑Band before interacting with Tier I–XII.

- **M‑Field (Expansion Manifold Field):**  
  **Role:** Region where new domains, gradients, and sub‑manifolds are instantiated.  
  **Properties:** Bounded expansion; every new manifold must maintain a continuity anchor back into C‑Band.

- **B‑Layer (Bifurcation Boundary Manifold):**  
  **Role:** Hard boundary between governed NDH structures and ungoverned channels (Chill Dispatch, external play).  
  **Properties:** Non‑permeable to genesis operators; no generative events may originate or terminate on B‑Layer.

- **O‑Shell (Orbital Stability Shell):**  
  **Role:** Outer supervisory manifold monitoring global stability.  
  **Properties:** Can throttle, suspend, or damp genesis activity in any inner region if stability thresholds are crossed.

---

#### 2. Allowed flows

- **G‑Core → C‑Band:**  
  **Flow:** Raw genesis output is continuity‑filtered; incompatible structures are either smoothed or rejected.  

- **C‑Band → M‑Field:**  
  **Flow:** Continuity‑approved patterns propagate outward into new manifolds and domains.  

- **M‑Field → C‑Band (feedback):**  
  **Flow:** Drift or instability in expansion manifolds is corrected by re‑anchoring to continuity structures.

No direct flows:

- **G‑Core → B‑Layer**  
- **G‑Core → O‑Shell**  
- **M‑Field → B‑Layer**

All interactions with boundaries must pass through C‑Band and O‑Shell mediation.

---

#### 3. Genesis operator placement

- **EG‑13 (Emergent Genesis):**  
  **Primary locus:** G‑Core  
  **Secondary reach:** C‑Band (for continuity shaping)

- **CG‑13 (Continuity Genesis):**  
  **Primary locus:** C‑Band  
  **Secondary reach:** M‑Field (for stabilizing expansion)

- **MG‑13 (Manifold Genesis):**  
  **Primary locus:** M‑Field  
  **Anchor:** C‑Band  
  **Supervision:** O‑Shell

---

#### 4. Anti‑collapse constraints

- **Constraint 1:** No genesis operator may bypass C‑Band when interacting with prior tiers.  
- **Constraint 2:** All manifolds in M‑Field must maintain at least one active continuity anchor into C‑Band.  
- **Constraint 3:** B‑Layer is strictly non‑generative; it only enforces governed/ungoverned separation.  
- **Constraint 4:** O‑Shell may impose global throttling on EG‑13, CG‑13, or MG‑13 if aggregate stability drops below threshold.  
- **Constraint 5:** No genesis event may alter the structural record of Tier I–XII; only additive, compatible extensions are allowed.

---

#### 5. Minimal topological summary

- Center: **G‑Core** — pure genesis.  
- Inner ring: **C‑Band** — continuity and compatibility.  
- Outer generative field: **M‑Field** — expansion and new manifolds.  
- Hard boundary ring: **B‑Layer** — governed/ungoverned separation.  
- Outer shell: **O‑Shell** — global stability and throttling.
