# **NDH_Genesis_Flow_Diagram_v1.0**  
### *Tier XIII — Post‑Structural Genesis Flow Topology*

This document defines the formal flow topology of genesis activity within Tier XIII. It describes the origin, stabilization, expansion, boundaries, and supervisory structures that govern post‑structural emergence. It is continuous with the Genesis Manifold Map and adheres to all Tier XIII stability constraints.

---

## **I. Genesis Flow Origin — G‑Core**

**Operator:** EG‑13 (Emergent Genesis)  
**Role:** Sole origin of genesis activity.  
**Properties:**  
- Non‑dual emergence  
- Non‑hierarchical generative field  
- High‑density genesis  
- Tightly bounded region  

**Flow Rule:**  
All genesis originates in G‑Core. No other region may initiate genesis.

---

## **II. Genesis Flow Stabilization — C‑Band**

**Operator:** CG‑13 (Continuity Genesis)  
**Role:** Stabilization and compatibility layer.  
**Properties:**  
- Smooths raw genesis  
- Enforces continuity with Tier I–XII  
- Corrects discontinuities  
- Rejects incompatible emergence  

**Flow Rule:**  
No genesis may bypass C‑Band. All genesis must be continuity‑filtered before expansion.

---

## **III. Genesis Flow Expansion — M‑Field**

**Operator:** MG‑13 (Manifold Genesis)  
**Role:** Expansion and domain formation.  
**Properties:**  
- Generates new manifolds, gradients, and domains  
- Bounded expansion  
- Requires active continuity anchors  
- No runaway growth permitted  

**Flow Rule:**  
Expansion is allowed only when continuity anchors to C‑Band remain active.

---

## **IV. Genesis Flow Boundary — B‑Layer**

**Operator:** None  
**Role:** Governed/ungoverned separation.  
**Properties:**  
- Non‑generative  
- Non‑permeable  
- Hard boundary  
- No genesis may originate or terminate here  

**Flow Rule:**  
B‑Layer is strictly a boundary. No genesis flow may cross it.

---

## **V. Genesis Flow Supervision — O‑Shell**

**Operator:** None  
**Role:** Global stability monitoring.  
**Properties:**  
- Supervises all inner layers  
- Can throttle or suspend genesis  
- Prevents collapse or drift  
- Maintains Tier XIII stability envelope  

**Flow Rule:**  
O‑Shell may intervene at any time if stability thresholds drop.

## **VII. Flow Summary**

1. **G‑Core → C‑Band**  
   Raw genesis enters continuity shaping.

2. **C‑Band → M‑Field**  
   Stabilized genesis expands into new manifolds.

3. **M‑Field → C‑Band (feedback)**  
   Drift or instability is corrected through continuity anchors.

4. **C‑Band → G‑Core (optional refinement)**  
   Corrected structures may refine genesis patterns.

5. **O‑Shell → All Layers**  
   Supervisory stability enforcement.

6. **B‑Layer → No Flow**  
   Boundary remains sealed.
