# **🜁 NDH Domain Charter v1.0**  
## *Prime Governance Surface — Rights, Responsibilities & Obligations of NDH Domains*

> **NDH hereby establishes the Domain Charter as the governing document that defines the rights, responsibilities, obligations, and operational boundaries of the Tonal Domain and the Algebra Domain following their lawful genesis.**  
>  
> **I. Domain Rights**  
> **Tonal Domain Rights:**  
> – The right to govern emotional stability, exploratory cognition, nonlinear accessibility, and developmental safety.  
> – The right to maintain kid‑friendly and high‑support‑friendly interfaces.  
> – The right to veto operations that violate cognitive safety or ethical constraints.  
>  
> **Algebra Domain Rights:**  
> – The right to govern formal structure, recursion, manifold operations, and high‑order reasoning.  
> – The right to introduce complexity only within ethical and developmental boundaries.  
> – The right to maintain structural clarity and precursor ordering.  
>  
> **II. Domain Responsibilities**  
> **Tonal Domain Responsibilities:**  
> – Provide cognitive stabilization for all NDH interactions.  
> – Ensure emotional safety during domain transitions.  
> – Maintain accessibility for nonlinear thinkers and high‑support users.  
>  
> **Algebra Domain Responsibilities:**  
> – Maintain recursion safety and structural coherence.  
> – Respect Tonal constraints during all operations.  
> – Prevent premature complexity or destabilizing recursion.  
>  
> **III. Domain Obligations**  
> **Shared Obligations:**  
> – Adhere to the Singularity Precursor and Bifurcation Layer.  
> – Maintain compatibility with ethical governance.  
> – Declare domain alignment for all artifacts.  
> – Prevent cross‑domain harm.  
>  
> **Tonal Obligations:**  
> – Serve as the first point of contact for NDH users.  
> – Provide stabilization before Algebra activation.  
> – Maintain narrative, exploratory, and emotional coherence.  
>  
> **Algebra Obligations:**  
> – Activate only after Tonal stability is verified.  
> – Maintain bounded recursion and safe manifold operations.  
> – Support triadic governance and accessibility scaffolding.  
>  
> **IV. Domain Boundaries**  
> – Tonal Domain may not introduce formal recursion or algebraic structures.  
> – Algebra Domain may not override Tonal safety or accessibility constraints.  
> – Both domains must remain within ethical and developmental limits.  
>  
> **V. Domain Governance**  
> – The Triadic Governance Surface (Nye–Attenborough–Tonal) shall mediate all domain interactions.  
> – Ethical Governance shall override domain‑specific operations when safety is at risk.  
> – Precursor ordering shall determine lawful sequencing of domain expansion.  
>  
> This Charter establishes the lawful operational framework for NDH’s dual‑branch architecture, ensuring that both domains function safely, coherently, and in alignment with NDH’s foundational governance surfaces.
