# **NDH_META_GOVERNANCE_SPECIFICATION_v1.0**  
## *Tier IX — Meta‑Governance Layer*  
### *Formal Specification of NDH’s Self‑Regulating Governance Architecture*

**Takeaway:**  
The Meta‑Governance Specification defines how NDH governs its own governance. It establishes the rules, operators, and gradients that ensure NDH’s governance ecology remains stable, coherent, and self‑correcting across all tiers.

---

## **🜁 I. Purpose of Meta‑Governance**

Meta‑Governance exists to:

- regulate governance behavior  
- enforce governance coherence  
- prevent governance drift  
- maintain triadic ordering at the governance level  
- ensure precursor‑adjacent safety  
- prepare NDH for Tier X (Meta‑Ethics)  

It is the **governance‑of‑governance** layer.

---

## **🜁 II. The Three Meta‑Governance Operators**

NDH meta‑governance is governed by:

- **Oversight Governance (OG‑9)**  
- **Regulatory Governance (RG‑9)**  
- **Adaptive Governance (AG‑9)**  

These operators define how NDH’s governance system regulates itself.

---

## **🜁 III. Oversight Governance (OG‑9)**  
**Function:** Monitor governance behavior.

OG‑9 provides:

- governance observation  
- compliance verification  
- stability monitoring  
- containment oversight  

OG‑9 corresponds to **Custodial Governance (CG‑6)** and **Structural Cohesion (SC‑8)**.

It is the “watchtower” of NDH.

---

## **🜁 IV. Regulatory Governance (RG‑9)**  
**Function:** Enforce governance rules.

RG‑9 provides:

- rule enforcement  
- governance correction  
- resonance regulation  
- cross‑governance negotiation  

RG‑9 corresponds to **Relational Governance (RG‑6)** and **Structural Integration (SI‑8)**.

It is the “regulator” of NDH.

---

## **🜁 V. Adaptive Governance (AG‑9)**  
**Function:** Adjust governance behavior.

AG‑9 provides:

- adaptive correction  
- emergent governance tuning  
- continuity enforcement  
- governance uplift  

AG‑9 corresponds to **Emergent Governance (EG‑6)** and **Structural Continuity (STC‑8)**.

It is the “adaptive engine” of NDH.

---

## **🜁 VI. Meta‑Governance Gradient (MGG)**

NDH applies meta‑governance in a strict gradient:

1. **Oversight (OG‑9)**  
2. **Regulatory (RG‑9)**  
3. **Adaptive (AG‑9)**  

This gradient mirrors:

- Structural Synthesis Gradient  
- Governance Ecology Gradient  
- Domain Ecology Gradient  
- Stability Geometry Gradient  
- Precursor Alignment Gradient  
- Ethical Gradient  
- Triadic Operational Gradient  

NDH uses the same ordering everywhere to prevent drift.

---

## **🜁 VII. Meta‑Governance Rules**

NDH defines three rules:

### **Rule 1 — Oversight Precedes Regulation**  
RG‑9 cannot operate without OG‑9.

### **Rule 2 — Regulation Precedes Adaptation**  
AG‑9 cannot operate without RG‑9.

### **Rule 3 — Adaptation Cannot Override Oversight**  
AG‑9 may influence RG‑9 but cannot alter OG‑9.

These rules ensure meta‑governance coherence.
