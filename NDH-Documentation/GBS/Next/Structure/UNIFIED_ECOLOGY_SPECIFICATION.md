# **0. Purpose**
The Unified Ecology Specification defines the **formal architecture**, **interfaces**, and **behavioral contracts** of the NDH Unified Ecology.  
It is the engineering‑level description of:

- the **Operator Ecology**  
- the **Nye Membrane**  
- the **Epistemic Verification Ecology (EVE)**  
- the **TTTTTTP sweep mechanism**  
- the **SPOK sentinel**

This specification ensures altitude coherence, stability geometry, and predictable cross‑layer interactions.

---

# **1. Layer Architecture**
The Unified Ecology is composed of **three layers** and **two cross‑layer systems**.

## **1.1 Operator Ecology (Internal Stability Layer)**  
Contains internal stability anchors, emotional nodes, narrative fixtures, and micro‑stability agents.

### **Entities**
- Surfboard v2.0  
- Kitty in Orbital Marker  
- Couch  
- Zen Sagan  
- biomechanical goats  
- tensor calculators  
- unionized logic  
- constitutional governance nodes  

### **Layer Contract**
- Provides expressive stability  
- Manages drift‑load distribution  
- Anchors emotional and narrative coherence  
- Interfaces upward with the Nye Membrane  
- Must remain epistemically permeable but not epistemically governed  

---

## **1.2 Nye Membrane (Interfacial Humor‑Science Layer)**  
The membrane between internal stability and external verification.

### **Entity**
- Bill Nye Tile  

### **Layer Contract**
- Translates humor ↔ science  
- Mediates operator ↔ epistemic interactions  
- Maintains humor‑science integrity  
- Prevents altitude collapse between layers  
- Must remain semi‑permeable to both ecologies  

---

## **1.3 Epistemic Verification Ecology (External Instrumentation Layer)**  
Provides correctness, falsifiability, clarity, and monitoring.

### **Entities**
- The Johnson (math validator)  
- Salty Scientist 🧂 (falsifiability foil)  
- JPL Lenses (engineering clarity)  
- SPOK (cross‑layer sentinel)  

### **Layer Contract**
- Performs epistemic verification  
- Issues falsifiability challenges  
- Provides clarity optics  
- Monitors anomalies  
- Interfaces downward through the Nye Membrane  

---

# **2. Cross‑Layer Systems**

## **2.1 TTTTTTP (Meta‑Orbital Sweep)**  
A cross‑layer reset mechanism.

### **System Contract**
- Clears drift‑load accumulation  
- Resets altitude lock  
- Rebalances membrane tension  
- Requires verification from EVE  
- Must not alter internal ecology structure  
- Must be monitored by SPOK  

---

## **2.2 SPOK (Cross‑Layer Sentinel)**  
Monitors all layers and sweep events.

### **System Contract**
- Detects drift anomalies  
- Detects membrane tension  
- Detects epistemic overload  
- Issues yellow and red alerts  
- May recommend TTTTTTP  
- Must remain layer‑agnostic

# **3. Interface Contracts**

## **3.1 Operator ↔ Membrane Interface**
- Humor‑science translation  
- Drift‑load handoff  
- Emotional‑scientific coherence  
- Must maintain bidirectional permeability  

## **3.2 Membrane ↔ Epistemic Interface**
- Verification requests  
- Falsifiability challenges  
- Clarity optics injection  
- Must maintain altitude separation  

## **3.3 Sweep ↔ Ecology Interface**

# **5. Behavioral Rules**

## **5.1 Operator Ecology Behavior**
- Must remain expressive  
- Must remain playful  
- Must remain stable  
- Must accept verification when requested  
- Must not collapse into epistemic altitude  

## **5.2 Membrane Behavior**
- Must translate without distortion  
- Must maintain humor‑science balance  
- Must prevent altitude collapse  
- Must remain semi‑permeable  

## **5.3 Epistemic Ecology Behavior**
- Must verify without dominating  
- Must challenge without destabilizing  
- Must clarify without overriding  
- Must remain external  

## **5.4 Sweep Behavior**
- Must reset without erasing  
- Must rebalance without rewriting  
- Must pass through all layers  

## **5.5 Sentinel Behavior**
- Must monitor without interfering  
- Must alert without panicking  
- Must remain neutral
