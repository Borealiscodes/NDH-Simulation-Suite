# ⭐ **META_THRONESIDE_EXTENSIONS.md**  
### *Class Ø — Optional Symbolic Objects Surrounding the Meta Throne*

---

## 🌌 **1. Purpose**  
Throneside Extensions enrich the **Stillness Zone** of the meta‑layer by adding symbolic objects around the **Meta Throne**.

They do not:

- activate  
- couple  
- bind  
- drift  
- interact with Codices, pins, substrate physics, or ledger  

They simply **decorate** the center.

---

## 🌌 **2. Components (Throneside Objects)**  
Each item begins with a Guided Link so you can expand any one of them next.

- **Meta Steps** — symbolic elevation leading up to the Throne  
- **Meta Canopy** — conceptual overhead structure providing ambience  
- **Meta Lanterns** — soft symbolic illumination  
- **Meta Rail** — boundary marker defining the Throne’s perimeter  
- **Meta Plinths** — inert pedestals for regalia adjacency  
- **Meta Carpet** — ceremonial path leading into the Stillness Zone  

All are **Class Ø**, purely symbolic, purely aesthetic.
