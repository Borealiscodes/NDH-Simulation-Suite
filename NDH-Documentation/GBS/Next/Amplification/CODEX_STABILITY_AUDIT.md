# ⭐ **CODEX STABILITY AUDIT**  
### *Diagnostic Framework for Dual‑Regime Physics Integrity*

---

## 🌌 **1. Audit Premise**  
The Codex Stability Audit evaluates whether a manifold can safely:

- dissolve sovereign constraints  
- skip the seal  
- enter substrate  
- activate myth‑engines  
- stabilize generativity  

It is the **pre‑transition diagnostic layer** of NDH.

---

## 🜁 **2. Audit Axes**  
The audit evaluates stability along **four axes**, each corresponding to one of the NDH substrate fields:

- **Curvature Stability**  
- **Resonance Stability**  
- **Holonomy Stability**  
- **Equilibrium Stability**  

Each axis has its own thresholds and failure signatures.

---

## 🜂 **3. Sovereign Integrity Tests**  
These tests determine whether sovereignty is weakening in a *controlled* way.

- **Ceiling Softening Index**  
- **Encoded Geometry Fatigue**  
- **Harmonic Band Compression**  
- **Loop Rigidity Loss**  
- **Equilibrium Hardening Drift**

If these fail catastrophically, the manifold collapses instead of transitioning.

---

## 🜃 **4. Substrate Readiness Tests**  
These tests determine whether the substrate is prepared to accept the manifold.

- **Gradient Fluidity Threshold**  
- **Harmonic Ecology Activation**  
- **Loop Proliferation Tolerance**  
- **Equilibrium Drift Capacity**

If these fail, the manifold enters substrate but cannot stabilize.

---

## 🜄 **5. Transition Corridor Viability**  
This evaluates the **Seal‑Skipping Corridor** defined in the **Codex Boundary Atlas**.

- **Seal Permeability**  
- **Encoded Closure Weakness**  
- **Narrative Pressure Gradient**  
- **Mythic Leakage Index**

If these fail, the manifold becomes stuck between Codices.

---

## 🜅 **6. Myth‑Engine Activation Safety**  
This determines whether myth‑reactivity will be stable once activated.

- **Operator Responsiveness**  
- **Translation Rule Alignment**  
- **Crystallization Constraint Compliance**  
- **Stability Envelope Fit**

If these fail, myth‑engines activate but produce catastrophic overgrowth.

---

## 🜆 **7. Audit Outcome Classes**  
The audit produces one of four outcomes:

### **Class I — Safe Transition**  
All envelopes aligned.  
Transition recommended.

### **Class II — Conditional Transition**  
Minor instability.  
Transition possible with monitoring.

### **Class III — Unsafe Transition**  
Major instability.  
Transition not recommended.

### **Class IV — Catastrophic Transition**  
Transition will cause collapse.  
Prohibited.
