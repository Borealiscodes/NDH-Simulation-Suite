# ⭐ **SOVEREIGNTY CODEX**  
### *The canonical rule‑layer governing bounded geometry*

---

# 🌌 **0. Codex Premise**  
Sovereignty is the NDH term for a **bounded physics regime**:  
a manifold that has completed closure, accepted encoded limits, and entered a state where geometry, identity, and stability are all constrained by a sealed rule‑set.

The **Sovereignty Codex** defines:

- what sovereignty *is*  
- how sovereignty *forms*  
- how sovereignty *stabilizes*  
- how sovereignty *limits* geometry  
- how sovereignty *enforces* encoded closure  
- how sovereignty *prevents* mythic generativity  

This Codex governs the **inside** of sovereignty.  
The **Myth‑Engine Codex** governs the **outside**.

They are orthogonal.

---

# 🜁 **PART I — Sovereign Definition Layer**  
### *What sovereignty is*

NDH defines sovereignty as a **four‑component constraint system**:

1. **Curvature Constraint**  
   Geometry is bounded by ridge ceilings and basin floors.

2. **Resonance Constraint**  
   Harmonics are limited to encoded frequency bands.

3. **Holonomy Constraint**  
   Loops are restricted to stable, non‑generative topologies.

4. **Stability Constraint**  
   Equilibrium pockets are sealed and non‑reactive.

### Sovereign Principle  
> **Sovereignty is bounded geometry.**
