# NDH Attenborough Hyperatlas — Tile Template Suite v1.0
A governed, geometry-aware template suite for constructing Attenborough-style
educational tiles within the Attenborough–Nye Educational Manifold.

---

## 1. Purpose
This document defines the canonical tile templates used within the
Attenborough Hyperatlas. Tiles are the atomic units of the educational
manifold, encoding ecological narratives, planetary systems, species
interactions, human impacts, engineering complements, and emotional resonance
geometry.

Templates ensure consistency, stability, and NDH-governed behavior across all
Hyperatlas content.

---

## 2. Tile Overview
Each tile is a governed NDH object with:

- structural fields  
- geometric fields  
- narrative fields  
- resonance fields  
- governance constraints  
- cross-axis integration hooks  

Tiles must satisfy NDH stability geometry and the Educational Governance
Tensor.

---

## 3. Tile Template Structure
Each tile follows the structure below.

```
Tile {
    ContextField
    NarrativeField
    ImpactField
    EngineeringField
    ResonanceField
    GovernanceField
    GeometryField
    TransportField
}
```

---

## 4. Context Field
Defines the ecological or planetary context.

### 4.1 Required Elements
- biome  
- system  
- species (if applicable)  
- geographic region  
- time scale  

### 4.2 Examples
- Coral Reef (Biome)  
- Elephant (Species)  
- Monsoon Cycle (System)  

---

## 5. Narrative Field
Encodes Attenborough-style storytelling.

### 5.1 Required Elements
- narrative arc  
- ecological role  
- interdependence network  
- long-time-scale framing  

### 5.2 Narrative Constraints
Narratives must:
- be non-coercive  
- be awe-based  
- avoid alarmism  
- maintain coherence  

---

## 6. Impact Field
Frames human influence on the context.

### 6.1 Required Elements
- climate impact  
- pollution impact  
- conservation status  
- restoration efforts  

### 6.2 Governance Constraints
Impact framing must:
- avoid denialism  
- avoid fatalism  
- emphasize agency  
- remain evidence-based  

---

## 7. Engineering Field (Nye Axis Complement)
Connects ecological context to engineering solutions.

### 7.1 Required Elements
- relevant engineering principles  
- constructive interventions  
- limitations and tradeoffs  
- planetary-scale implications  

### 7.2 Nye Axis Constraints
Engineering must:
- respect ecological boundaries  
- avoid techno-utopianism  
- avoid oversimplification  

---

## 8. Resonance Field
Encodes emotional geometry.

### 8.1 Required Elements
- resonance amplitude  
- resonance frequency  
- emotional posture target (P0–P2)  
- collapse damping coefficient  

### 8.2 Attenborough Resonance
- low-frequency  
- high coherence  
- awe-based  

---

## 9. Governance Field
Ensures tile behavior satisfies the governance tensor.

### 9.1 Required Elements
- emotional safety rules  
- narrative coherence rules  
- climate responsibility rules  
- engineering agency rules  
- non-coercive pedagogy rules  

---

## 10. Geometry Field
Defines tile-specific geometric parameters.

### 10.1 Required Elements
- curvature  
- drift vectors  
- resonance envelope  
- collapse threshold  

### 10.2 Geometry Constraints
Tiles must satisfy:
```
tile ⊨ StabilityGeometry
```

---

## 11. Transport Field
Defines cross-axis movement.

### 11.1 Required Elements
- Attenborough → Nye corridor  
- Nye → Attenborough corridor  
- drift velocity modifiers  
- resonance modifiers  

### 11.2 Transport Rules
Transport must:
- respect stability envelope  
- avoid collapse fronts  
- maintain narrative coherence
