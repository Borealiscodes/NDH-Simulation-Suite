A governed expansion‑governance architecture for the NDH Multi‑Domain Atlas.

domain‑growth ethics, cross‑domain expansion boundaries, lineage‑anchored
governance fields, and non‑activation guarantees.

## 1. Purpose
v3.3 establishes the governance system for the NDH atlas during geometric
expansion.  
It ensures that multi‑domain expansion remains:

- ethically coherent  
- lineage‑anchored  
- identity‑preserving  
- emotionally safe  
- non‑coercive  
- non‑activating  

This spec continues the NDH v3.x evolution‑architecture phase.

## 2. Expansion Governance Architecture Overview
The atlas expansion‑governance system consists of six layers:

1. **Domain Expansion‑Governance Layer**  
2. **Cross‑Domain Expansion‑Governance Layer**  
3. **Atlas‑Wide Expansion‑Governance Layer**  
4. **Lineage Expansion‑Governance Layer**  
5. **Ethical Expansion‑Constraint Layer**

Each layer enforces strict boundaries to prevent destabilizing geometric
growth, identity bleed, collapse, or unbounded resonance amplification.
