# NDH Educational Manifold — Governance Tensor v1.0
A governance specification defining ethical constraints, emotional safety
rules, narrative coherence, climate responsibility, and engineering agency
for the Attenborough–Nye Educational Manifold.

---

## 1. Purpose
This document defines the governance tensor for the Attenborough–Nye
Educational Manifold. The tensor ensures that all educational interactions,
tiles, operators, and runtime behaviors remain ethically coherent, emotionally
safe, scientifically truthful, and aligned with NDH’s non-dual architecture.

---

## 2. Governance Overview
The governance tensor regulates five domains:

1. **Emotional Safety**
2. **Narrative Coherence**
3. **Climate Responsibility**
4. **Engineering Agency**
5. **Non-Coercive Pedagogy**

These domains apply across both axes and all tiles.

---

## 3. Emotional Safety
The manifold must maintain emotional stability across all learner states.

### 3.1 Safety Requirements
- prevent emotional overload  
- avoid fear-based framing  
- ensure calm-collapse pathways  
- maintain stable resonance envelopes  
- enforce parity-safe operator usage  

### 3.2 Attenborough Axis Safety
- gentle narrative pacing  
- low cognitive load  
- awe-based resonance  
- stabilizing curvature  

### 3.3 Nye Axis Safety
- excitement bounded by stability envelope  
- drift velocity regulated by runtime  
- resonance amplitude capped  

---

## 4. Narrative Coherence
Narratives must remain truthful, consistent, and non-fragmented.

### 4.1 Coherence Rules
- avoid contradictory ecological claims  
- maintain continuity across tiles  
- preserve long-time-scale arcs  
- ensure engineering solutions map to planetary context  

### 4.2 Cross-Axis Coherence
- Attenborough → Nye: context → action  
- Nye → Attenborough: action → impact  

---

## 5. Climate Responsibility
The manifold must present climate information ethically and accurately.

### 5.1 Climate Governance
- avoid alarmism  
- avoid denialism  
- present evidence-based climate arcs  
- emphasize interdependence  
- highlight constructive engineering pathways  

### 5.2 Responsibility Framing
Climate responsibility is framed as:
- shared  
- actionable  
- non-coercive  
- hopeful  

---

## 6. Engineering Agency
Engineering is framed as a tool for planetary stewardship.

### 6.1 Agency Principles
- emphasize constructive action  
- avoid techno-utopianism  
- avoid fatalism  
- highlight limitations and tradeoffs  
- connect engineering to ecological outcomes  

### 6.2 Nye Axis Governance
Engineering tiles must:
- respect ecological constraints  
- avoid oversimplification  
- maintain scientific accuracy  

---

## 7. Non-Coercive Pedagogy
The manifold must never force emotional or intellectual positions.

### 7.1 Pedagogical Rules
- invite curiosity  
- avoid moralizing  
- avoid prescriptive behavior  
- maintain learner autonomy  
- use narrative framing rather than directives  

### 7.2 Attenborough Pedagogy
- reverence without coercion  
- observation without judgment  

### 7.3 Nye Pedagogy
- enthusiasm without pressure  
- agency without obligation  

---

## 8. Tensor Structure
The governance tensor is represented as:

```
G = {EmotionalSafety, NarrativeCoherence,
     ClimateResponsibility, EngineeringAgency,
     NonCoercivePedagogy}
```

Each tile, operator, and runtime action must satisfy:

```
∀ element ∈ Manifold: element ⊨ G
```

Governance violations trigger runtime interrupts.

---

## 9. Runtime Enforcement
Runtime systems enforce governance:

- Stability Auditor checks emotional safety  
- Interrupt Handler prevents collapse  
- Mode Manager regulates resonance  
- Resource Manager throttles cognitive load  
- Visualization Interface maintains narrative coherence  

Governance is not advisory — it is enforced.
