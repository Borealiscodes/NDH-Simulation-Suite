# NDH Educational Manifold — Attenborough–Nye Architecture v1.0
A first‑class NDH domain defining the dual‑axis educational manifold built
from the Attenborough planetary calm axis and the Nye engineering energetic
axis.

---

## 1. Purpose
This architecture establishes the Attenborough–Nye Educational Manifold as a
governed NDH domain. It defines the domain geometry, stability envelope,
transport corridors, governance tensor, runtime hooks, and cross‑axis
integration rules required for full NDH compatibility.

---

## 2. Domain Overview
The manifold consists of two complementary axes:

### Attenborough Axis (Planetary Calm)
- planetary‑scale ecological systems  
- long‑time‑scale narratives  
- calming tonal geometry  
- biodiversity and interdependence  
- emotional stability and reverence  

### Nye Axis (Engineering Energetic)
- human‑scale engineering  
- physics and chemistry fundamentals  
- energetic curiosity resonance  
- problem‑solving pathways  
- agency and constructive action  

Together they form a **non‑dual educational ecology**.

---

## 3. Domain Geometry
The manifold geometry is defined by:

### 3.1 Curvature Fields
- **Attenborough curvature:** low‑frequency, stable, wide‑aperture  
- **Nye curvature:** high‑frequency, energetic, narrow‑aperture  

### 3.2 Drift Vectors
- curiosity drift along both axes  
- cross‑axis drift through narrative → engineering corridors  

### 3.3 Resonance Envelopes
- Attenborough resonance: awe, calm, reflection  
- Nye resonance: excitement, momentum, experimentation  

### 3.4 Collapse Fronts
- cognitive overload  
- emotional stress  
- narrative fragmentation  

Collapse fronts are mitigated through calm collapse operators.

---

## 4. Stability Envelope
The domain’s stability envelope regulates learner emotional and cognitive
load:

- **P0:** calm, receptive, stable  
- **P1:** curious, drifting  
- **P2:** excited, resonant  
- **P3:** overwhelmed, collapse‑prone  

Attenborough axis naturally stabilizes P0–P1.  
Nye axis naturally energizes P1–P2.

### 5.2 Engineering Corridors

### 5.3 Emotional Corridors

These corridors prevent drift and collapse.
