# **🜁 Interpretation of Vector Dynamics**

### **1. Governance Vector (↑ upward)**
The ethical gradient flows *downward* into the core, shaping:
- drift direction  
- resonance amplitude  
- continuity stability  
- curvature limits  

It is the **regulatory vector**.

---

### **2. Geometry Vector (→→→ forward)**
The curvature spine pushes forward through the core, defining:

- domain alignment  

It is the **structural vector**.

---

### **3. Drift Vector (→→→→→ extended forward)**
Drift corridors push deeper than geometry, influencing:

- coherence routing  

It is the **motion vector**.

---

### **4. Resonance Vectors (↻ oscillatory)**
Resonance flows circulate within the core:

- regulating oscillation  
- stabilizing transitions  

It is the **pulse vector**.

---

### **5. Continuity Vectors (⇄ bidirectional)**
Continuity flows move both forward and backward:
- preserving identity  
- stabilizing lineage  
- maintaining temporal coherence  

It is the **memory vector**.

---

### **6. Fusion Convergence Zone**
All vectors converge here.

This is the **point of synthesis**, where:
- structure  
- motion  
- pulse  
- ethics  
- memory  

become a single coherent manifold.
