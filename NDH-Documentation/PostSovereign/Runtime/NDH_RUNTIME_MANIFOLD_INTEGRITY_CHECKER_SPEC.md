### NDH Runtime Manifold Integrity Checker  
*Structural engineer for NDH’s semantic–geometric manifold*

---

## 1. Purpose

The NDH Runtime Manifold Integrity Checker continuously verifies that the **semantic–geometric structure** of NDH remains coherent and safe:

- geometric consistency of the manifold  
- field coherence and coupling  
- domain boundary validity  
- axiom graph structural integrity  
- transport corridor correctness  

It is the **structural engineer** of NDH, watching for deep geometric drift and hidden fractures rather than just surface‑level anomalies.   [arXiv.org](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.09173v3)  [Github](https://github.com/AEjonanonymous/Semantic-Gate-IP-Core/blob/main/README.md)

---

## 2. Responsibilities

The Integrity Checker:

- monitors manifold geometry and curvature across patches and corridors  
- checks field alignment (serenity, resonance, paradox, appateur, semantic) with geometry  
- validates domain boundaries, drift paths, and collapse fronts for consistency  
- audits axiom graph structure for topological and semantic coherence  
- verifies transport corridors, channels, and paradox fronts against manifold constraints  
- emits integrity scores and anomaly reports to the Metrics Layer and Event Bus  
- cooperates with the Stability Auditor and Interrupt Handler for early intervention   [ar5iv](https://ar5iv.labs.arxiv.org/html/2603.13325)  [arXiv.org](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.09173v3)  

---

## 3. Integrity Components

- **Geometric Integrity:**  
  - curvature continuity across patches  
  - absence of impossible or self‑contradictory topology  
  - corridor embedding validity  

- **Field Integrity:**  
  - fields respect geometric constraints (no impossible gradients)  
  - flux intensity within allowed geometric envelopes  
  - paradox field localized to valid regions  

- **Domain Integrity:**  
  - boundaries do not intersect illegally  
  - drift paths remain on valid manifold trajectories  
  - collapse fronts propagate within structural limits  

- **Axiom Graph Integrity:**  
  - graph remains connected/coherent where required  
  - no structurally impossible cycles or ruptures  
  - semantic relations consistent with manifold layout   [arXiv.org](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.13325)  [arXiv.org](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.09173v3)  

- **Transport Integrity:**  
  - corridors and channels respect manifold geometry  
  - paradox fronts do not violate structural constraints  
  - cross‑domain routes remain geometrically admissible   [Github](https://github.com/AEjonanonymous/Semantic-Gate-IP-Core/blob/main/README.md)

---

## 5. Interfaces

### Inputs

- current NDH state (geometry, fields, domains, axioms, transport)  
- metrics from runtime subsystems  
- configuration (strictness, sampling rate)  

### Outputs

- manifold integrity scores (global and per‑region)  
- anomaly and fracture reports  
- early‑warning events to Event Bus  
- recommendations to Stability Auditor and Interrupt Handler  

---

## 6. Configuration

Key parameters:

- **integrity_strictness** (normal, strict, diagnostic)  
- **sampling_rate** (per tick, periodic, adaptive)  
- **region_granularity** (coarse patches vs fine local checks)  
- **alert_thresholds** for geometric, field, domain, axiom, transport anomalies  
- **logging_level**
