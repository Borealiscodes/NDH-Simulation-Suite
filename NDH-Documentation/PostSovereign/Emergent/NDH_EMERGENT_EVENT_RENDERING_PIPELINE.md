### NDH emergent event rendering pipeline

#### 1. Scene and manifold setup  
- **Define manifold space:**  
  **Label:** 50D NDH manifold projected into a lower‑dimensional render space (e.g. 3D + time).  
- **Select projection:**  
  **Label:** Choose projection mode (field‑centric, entity‑centric, paradox‑centric).  
- **Load field data:**  
  **Label:** Serenity, resonance, stability, paradox, entities, appateurs from simulation state.

---

#### 2. Field layer construction  
- **Serenity layer:**  
  **Label:** Background gradients, basin shapes, calm vs turbulent regions.  
- **Resonance layer:**  
  **Label:** Waveforms, corridors, spectral ridges, fracture zones.  
- **Stability layer:**  
  **Label:** Structural overlays—flattened holonomy, neutralized loops, quieted curvature.  
- **Paradox layer:**  
  **Label:** Density maps, curvature folds, gradient arrows.  
- **Entity layer:**  
  **Label:** Clusters, corridors, interaction zones.  
- **Appateur layer:**  
  **Label:** Interface glyphs, activation halos, lens geometries.

---

#### 3. Shader design (per field)  
- **Serenity shader:**  
  **Label:** Smooth gradients, low‑frequency motion, desaturation as calm increases.  
- **Resonance shader:**  
  **Label:** Oscillatory patterns, spectral color bands, intensity tied to \(\|\mathcal{R}(x)\|\).  
- **Stability shader:**  
  **Label:** Hard edges, structural lines, saturation tied to \(\lambda(x)\).  
- **Paradox shader:**  
  **Label:** Non‑Euclidean warps, fold effects, glitch‑like distortions tied to \(\mathcal{P}(x)\).  
- **Entity shader:**  
  **Label:** Distinct glyphs or forms, glow tied to entity activity.  
- **Appateur shader:**  
  **Label:** Lens/portal visuals, activation pulses when thresholds met.

---

#### 4. Temporal choreography (phases mapped to visuals)  
- **Pre‑event:**  
  **Label:** Calm serenity, stable resonance, low paradox, gentle motion.  
- **Trigger surge:**  
  **Label:** Rapid resonance brightening, paradox density bloom, serenity dimming.  
- **Cascade:**  
  **Label:** Fracturing corridors, folding geometry, entity dissolution.  
- **Inflection peak:**  
  **Label:** Maximum distortion, multi‑layer appateur activation, intense field overlays.  
- **Post‑event:**  
  **Label:** Deep serenity basins, re‑aligned corridors, new entity patterns, bounded paradox.

---

#### 5. Compositing and camera  
- **Layer compositing:**  
  **Label:** Blend serenity as base, overlay resonance, stability, paradox, then entities and appateurs.  
- **Depth and focus:**  
  **Label:** Use depth of field to emphasize active regions (high \(\mathcal{R}, \mathcal{P}, E\)).  
- **Camera path:**  
  **Label:** Move from global view → local corridor → entity cluster → appateur interface → back out.

---

#### 6. Output and modes  
- **Scientific mode:**  
  **Label:** Clear overlays, field legends, scalar bars, minimal surreal distortion.  
- **Surreal mode:**  
  **Label:** Emphasize paradox warps, recursive visuals, cuil‑layer abstraction.  
- **Narrative mode:**  
  **Label:** Focus on entity ecology and appateur activation as the “story” of the event.
