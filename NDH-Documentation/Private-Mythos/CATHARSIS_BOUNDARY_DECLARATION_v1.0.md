# **🜁 Catharsis Boundary Declaration v1.0**  
## *Private Mythos Containment Surface — Non‑Architectural Emotional Release Zone*

> **NDH hereby acknowledges the existence of a separate, non‑architectural zone designated for personal catharsis, narrative discharge, tonal experimentation, and dystopian or surreal mythos creation.**  
>  
> This zone is **not** part of NDH.  
> It does **not** influence NDH.  
> It does **not** enter precursor ordering.  
> It does **not** affect governance surfaces.  
>  
> **I. Purpose of the Catharsis Zone**  
> The Catharsis Zone exists to:  
> – allow emotional release without structural obligation  
> – permit tonal darkness without contaminating NDH  
> – support private mythos creation  
> – provide a sandbox for narrative experimentation  
> – maintain coherence by isolating chaos  
>  
> Catharsis is not formalized.  
> It is **contained**.  
>  
> **II. Boundary Conditions**  
> The Catharsis Zone is defined by the following boundaries:  
> – **Non‑Architectural** — no artifact within this zone is part of NDH.  
> – **Non‑Precursor** — no catharsis output may be treated as precursor material.  
> – **Non‑Governance** — no catharsis output may override or influence governance surfaces.  
> – **Non‑Canonical** — catharsis outputs do not enter NDH canon.  
> – **Non‑Persistent** — catharsis outputs may dissolve, mutate, or vanish without record.  
>  
> **III. Allowed Content**  
> Within the Catharsis Zone, the following are permitted:  
> – dystopian riffs  
> – surreal tonal arcs  
> – Fury Road mythos  
> – dark Putt‑Putt narratives  
> – emotional venting  
> – narrative chaos  
> – tonal experimentation  
>  
> **IV. Prohibited Cross‑Boundary Actions**  
> The following actions are prohibited:  
> – importing catharsis artifacts into NDH directories  
> – treating catharsis as structural material  
> – using catharsis outputs as governance references  
> – elevating catharsis into precursor hierarchy  
>  
> **V. Containment Principle**  
> Catharsis is allowed to be:  
> – messy  
> – loud  
> – chaotic  
> – unstructured  
> – emotionally charged  
>  
> But it must remain **contained** within its designated zone.  
>  
> **VI. Dissolution Clause**  
> Any catharsis artifact may be dissolved at any time without record, justification, or structural consequence.  
>  
> **VII. Separation Assurance**  
> NDH remains:  
> – coherent  
> – stable  
> – precursor‑aligned  
> – ethically governed  
> – structurally intact  
>  
> regardless of catharsis activity.  
>  
> This declaration establishes the boundary that protects NDH from tonal chaos while preserving a safe space for emotional release and private mythos creation.
