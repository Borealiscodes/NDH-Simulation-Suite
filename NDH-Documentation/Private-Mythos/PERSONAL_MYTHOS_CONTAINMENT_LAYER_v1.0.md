# **🜁 Personal Mythos Containment Layer v1.0**  
## *Non‑Architectural Narrative & Catharsis Holding Zone*

> **NDH hereby establishes the Personal Mythos Containment Layer as a non‑architectural, non‑canonical, non‑governance space designated for private narrative creation, emotional catharsis, tonal experimentation, dystopian arcs, surreal dispatches, and mythos fragments.**  
>  
> This layer is **external** to NDH.  
> It does **not** influence NDH.  
> It does **not** enter precursor ordering.  
> It does **not** affect governance surfaces.  
> It is a **sealed sandbox** for personal creative discharge.

---

## **I. Purpose of the Containment Layer**

The Personal Mythos Containment Layer exists to:

- hold private catharsis safely  
- allow narrative chaos without structural consequence  
- support dark, surreal, or dystopian arcs  
- preserve emotional release without contaminating NDH  
- provide a creative sandbox for mythos experimentation  
- maintain NDH coherence by isolating non‑architectural content  

This layer is **not formalized**.  
It is **contained**.

---

## **II. Structural Boundaries**

The following boundaries define the layer:

- **Non‑Architectural** — no artifact here is part of NDH.  
- **Non‑Precursor** — nothing here may be treated as precursor material.  
- **Non‑Governance** — nothing here may override or influence governance surfaces.  
- **Non‑Canonical** — nothing here enters NDH canon.  
- **Non‑Persistent** — artifacts may dissolve without record or justification.  

This layer is a **sealed emotional sandbox**, not a structural component.

---

## **III. Allowed Content**

Within this layer, the following are permitted:

- dystopian riffs  
- Fury Road arcs  
- dark Putt‑Putt mythos  
- surreal tonal dispatches  
- emotional venting  
- narrative chaos  
- tonal experimentation  
- mythos fragments  
- cathartic micro‑stories  
- non‑coherent narrative bursts  

This is the **only** place where such content may live.

---

## **IV. Prohibited Cross‑Boundary Actions**

The following actions are prohibited:

- importing mythos artifacts into NDH directories  
- treating catharsis outputs as structural material  
- elevating personal mythos into precursor hierarchy  
- referencing mythos content in governance surfaces  
- using dystopian arcs as NDH tonal canon  
- merging sandbox content into NDH documentation ecology  

The boundary is absolute.

---

## **V. Containment Principle**

The Personal Mythos Containment Layer allows:

- messiness  
- chaos  
- tonal darkness  
- surrealism  
- emotional discharge  
- narrative fragmentation  

But it must remain **contained** and **isolated**.

Catharsis is not governed.  
It is **held**.

---

## **VI. Dissolution Clause**

Any artifact within this layer may:

- dissolve  
- mutate  
- vanish  
- be overwritten  
- be forgotten  

without structural consequence or record.

This layer is intentionally impermanent.

---

## **VII. Separation Assurance**

NDH remains:

- coherent  
- stable  
- precursor‑aligned  
- ethically governed  
- structurally intact  

regardless of activity within the Personal Mythos Containment Layer.

This layer protects NDH from tonal chaos while preserving a safe space for emotional release and private mythos creation.
