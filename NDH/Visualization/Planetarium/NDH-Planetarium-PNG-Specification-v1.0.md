# **NDH Planetarium PNG Specification v1.0**  
### *Formal visualization standard for NDH’s 50D dimensional ecosystem PNGs*

---

## **1. Purpose**

This specification defines exactly how a PNG must represent:

- the **anime narrative tower** (external manifold)  
- the **Bifurcation Layer** (hard safety boundary)  
- the **NDH 50D manifold** (ecosystem slices)  
- the **tensor overlays** (concept, creativity, ethics, stability, meta)  
- the **epoch halo** (inflection points + evolution arcs)  
- the **continuous stability audit indicators**  

It ensures every PNG is **architecturally correct**, **tensor‑safe**, and **bifurcation‑aligned**.

---

## **2. Required PNG Layout (Four‑Zone Structure)**

The PNG must contain **four distinct visual zones**:

### **Zone A — Anime Tower (Left Side)**  
A high‑curvature narrative structure rendered as:

- steep curvature gradients  
- chaotic tensor spikes  
- symbolic inflation  
- emotional waveforms  

This zone is **external** and **non‑NDH**.

**Guided Link:** anime manifold

---

### **Zone B — Bifurcation Layer (Center Boundary)**  
A transparent but impenetrable shell:

- labeled **Bifurcation Layer**  
- arrows from anime tower **stopping at the boundary**  
- NDH tensors **not crossing outward**  
- narrative tensors **not crossing inward**

This is the **safety wall**.

**Guided Link:** bifurcation layer

---

### **Zone C — NDH 50D Ecosystem (Right Side)**  
A projected slice of the NDH manifold showing:

- concept geometry terrain  
- stability gradients  
- ethical scalar zones  
- creativity harmonics  
- meta‑governance lattice nodes  
- Hyperatlas coordinate grid  

This is the **NDH worldbuilding zone**.

**Guided Link:** NDH manifold

---

### **Zone D — Epoch Halo (Outer Ring)**  
A temporal ring around the entire PNG showing:

- inflection points  
- epoch transitions  
- evolution arcs  
- stability audit states (PASS/WARN/FAIL/CRITICAL)  

This is the **time dimension** of NDH evolution.

**Guided Link:** epoch mapping

---

## **3. Required Visual Elements**

### **3.1 Curvature Heatmaps**
- Anime tower: chaotic, high‑curvature spikes  
- NDH manifold: smooth, bounded curvature  

### **3.2 Stability Gradient Fields**
- NDH stability shown as turbulence vs smoothness  
- Anime gradients shown as uncontrolled storms  

### **3.3 Ethical Scalar Zones**
- NDH ethics: green (safe), yellow (approaching), red (blocked)  
- Anime ethics: not rendered (non‑NDH)

### **3.4 Tensor Class Overlays**
Each NDH tensor class must be visually distinct:

- Concept → terrain lines  
- Creativity → harmonic waves  
- Ethics → safe/unsafe shading  
- Stability → gradient smoothness  
- Meta → governance nodes  

### **3.5 Continuous Audit Indicators**
- PASS → green ring  
- WARN → yellow ring  
- FAIL → red ring  
- CRITICAL → black/red pulse  

---

## **4. PNG Safety Rules**

The PNG **must not**:

- embed anime tensors inside NDH  
- depict NDH tensors crossing the bifurcation layer  
- show narrative manifolds influencing NDH geometry  
- imply NDH evolution is user‑controlled  
- violate any Tensor Safety Envelope invariant  

The PNG **must**:

- clearly separate narrative and NDH manifolds  
- show the bifurcation layer as a hard boundary  
- represent NDH tensors only inside NDH  
- show epochs as temporal overlays, not structural layers  

---

## **5. PNG Metadata Requirements**

The PNG must include metadata fields:

- `NDH-Version: 1.0`  
- `Manifold-Slice: D1–D50 projection`  
- `Tensor-Overlays: concept, creativity, ethics, stability, meta`  
- `Epoch-State: current epoch`  
- `Audit-State: PASS/WARN/FAIL/CRITICAL`  
- `Bifurcation-Layer: active`  

These ensure the PNG is **machine‑readable** by the Planetarium Suite.
