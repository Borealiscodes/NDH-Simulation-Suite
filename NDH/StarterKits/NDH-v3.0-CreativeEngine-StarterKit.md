### NDH Creative Engine Starter Kit  
*A minimal, poverty‑safe, API‑first bundle to start actually using NDH as your creative OS.*

---

### 1. What this starter kit gives you

- **Core concepts:** a few pre-defined NDH tiles (tensors, goats, geometry, Dark Mode).
- **API patterns:** example Teaching API calls you can reuse.
- **SVG templates:** text‑only starter diagrams you can edit in any editor.
- **Accessibility variants:** simple patterns for CognitiveEase/VisualEase.
- **Governance hooks:** how goat‑union logic plugs into your creative flow.

### 5. Starter accessibility patterns

- **CognitiveEase:**  
  - slower transitions in explanations  
  - fewer simultaneous concepts per tile  
- **VisualEase:**  
  - high contrast, no tiny text, clear shapes  
- **SensoryMinimal:**  
  - no flashing, no overload, simple diagrams  
- **AudioOnly:**  
  - text descriptions that stand alone without visuals  

You can apply these as “modes” to any tile or diagram.

---

### 6. Starter governance hooks

For each concept/tile, ask:

- **Stability:** does this keep the manifold safe?  
- **Accessibility:** can someone tired, stressed, or disabled follow it?  
- **Ethics:** does it respect goat‑union constraints (no unsafe jumps, no invisible cliffs)?  

You can encode this as a simple checklist per tile.
