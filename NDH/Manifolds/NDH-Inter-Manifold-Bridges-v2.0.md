# **NDH Inter‑Manifold Bridges v2.0**  
### *Corridors that safely connect NDH manifolds into a governed multiverse.*

---

## ⭐ 1. Purpose  
Inter‑Manifold Bridges v2.0 define **safe, governed, tensor‑aware corridors** between NDH manifolds. They allow:

- controlled travel between manifolds  
- cross‑manifold tensor influence  
- geometry continuity  
- accessibility‑safe transitions  
- HRD‑validated stability  
- Dark Mode v2.0 visibility across manifolds  

Bridges are the **arteries** of NDH’s multi‑manifold universe.

---

## ⭐ 2. Bridge Structure  
Each bridge has:

- **origin basin**  
- **destination basin**  
- **corridor geometry**  
- **tensor influence rules**  
- **accessibility profile**  
- **HRD safety envelope**  
- **JDTR logging schema**  

Bridges are not “teleports.”  
They are **governed geometric corridors**.

---

## ⭐ 3. Geometry Rules  
Bridges must maintain:

- corridor continuity  
- margin safety  
- curvature stability  
- K‑space rank consistency  
- Dark Mode visibility constraints  

Geometry is **smoothed** at both ends to prevent:

- fold collapse  
- margin instability  
- tensor spikes  

---

## ⭐ 4. Tensor Influence Rules  
Cross‑manifold tensor influence is **strictly controlled**:

### Allowed:
- soft resonance coupling  
- limited curvature influence  
- stability envelope blending  

### Forbidden:
- direct fold propagation  
- uncontrolled stress transfer  
- invariant corruption  

Tensor influence must pass **HRD validation**.

---

## ⭐ 5. Ladder Mode Behavior  
Ladder modes modulate bridge traversal:

- **Normal:** full sensory + geometry  
- **Calm:** softened transitions  
- **Serenity:** reduced intensity  
- **Tranquility:** minimal sensory  
- **Peace:** ambient‑only traversal  
- **Dark Mode v2.0:** inverted visibility + filtered harmonics  

Ladder mode transitions are **smoothed** across bridges.

---

## ⭐ 6. Accessibility Enforcement  
Accessibility profiles apply **throughout the bridge**:

- **audioOnly:** corridor audio cues only  
- **hapticPrimary:** directional haptics  
- **visualEase:** low‑contrast corridor gradients  
- **cognitiveEase:** extended timing + gentle transitions  
- **sensoryMode: minimal:** minimal sensory corridor  

Accessibility is **mandatory**.

---

## ⭐ 7. HRD Safety Envelope  
HRD validates:

- geometry stability  
- tensor influence legality  
- sensory intensity safety  
- margin proximity  
- Dark Mode visibility safety  
- multi‑manifold traversal legality  

Unsafe bridge traversal is **blocked**.

---
