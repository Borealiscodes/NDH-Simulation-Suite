
# **NDH Geometry Visualizer v2.0**  
### *Real‑time visualization of basins, corridors, margins, K‑space, and manifold curvature.*

---

## ⭐ 1. Purpose  
The Geometry Visualizer v2.0 provides a **real‑time visual interface** for NDH’s geometry envelope:

- basins  
- corridors  
- margins  
- K‑space layers  
- manifold curvature  
- stability envelopes  
- Dark Mode visibility transforms  

It is the **primary geometric debugging tool** for NDH v2.0.
