---

## ⭐ **1. Definition**

**Serenity Mode** is a **mid‑depth equilibrium context** that stabilizes the NDH manifold more deeply than Calm Mode but without entering the full resting geometry of Peace Mode.

It provides:

- deep tensor quieting  
- luminous invariant stabilization  
- basin‑wide harmonic alignment  
- corridor soft‑flow geometry  
- chaos margin dormancy  
- resonance clarity  
- K‑space gentle coherence  

Serenity Mode is the NDH equivalent of **“the world is quiet, but alive.”**

---

## ⭐ **2. Activation Contract**

### **Endpoint:**  
**NDH.SerenityMode.Enable**

### Preconditions
- Calm Mode must be stable  
- No active chaos margin contact  
- No elevated fold tensor  
- HRD validation must return safe  
- Invariants must be bright  

### Postconditions
- geometry enters luminous stillness  
- resonance becomes clear and harmonic  
- stress tensors drop to minimal amplitude  
- fold tensors stabilize completely  
- chaos margins enter dormancy  
- K‑space aligns into soft coherence  

### State Change
```

```

## ⭐ **3. Deactivation Contract**

### **Endpoint:**  
**NDH.SerenityMode.Disable**

- no basin harmonic cycle in progress
