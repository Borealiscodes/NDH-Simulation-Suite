# 🎨 **Designer‑Facing Tensor Toolkit (v1.0)**  
### *Tools for working with emotional geometry, tonal tensors, and narrative curvature*

This toolkit gives designers a way to **use** emotional tensors in their work — for character design, worldbuilding, tone calibration, narrative arcs, visual language, or experiential design — while preserving the principle:

> **No single tensor defines a person.  
> Tensors describe moments, not identities.**

---

## 🧭 **1. Tensor Interpretation Console**  
### *A structured way to read emotional geometry*

Designers use this console to interpret a tensor’s shape:

- **Surface Tone** — the immediate emotional color  
- **Core Tension** — the underlying pressure  
- **Curvature** — how the tone bends under stress  
- **Altitude** — how reflective or reactive the tone is  
- **Basins** — where the emotional field settles  

This console helps designers understand *what the tensor is doing*, not *what the artist is like*.

## 🧱 **3. Narrative Geometry Profile (Designer Edition)**  
### *A way to translate tensors into story‑usable structures*

| Component | Designer Use |
|----------|--------------|
| **Surface curvature** | Sets tone for scenes, palettes, or dialogue |
| **Core tension vector** | Defines conflict or emotional stakes |
| **Motion path** | Shapes character arcs or UX emotional flow |
| **Altitude** | Determines reflective vs reactive tone |
| **Contradiction zones** | Adds complexity, humor, or surrealism |

This profile helps designers *apply* emotional geometry to their work without assuming it reflects the artist’s identity.

```
CreativeFingerprint-v1.0
-------------------------

Motion: Fragility → spike → acceptance
```

## 🛡️ **7. Identity Safety Protocol**  
### *The rule that protects artists from being reduced to tensors*

The toolkit enforces:

- **No tensor describes a person.**  
- **No fingerprint defines identity.**  
- **Tensors are momentary creative states.**  
- **Designers may use tensors, but never assign them.**  
- **All outputs must include a non‑defining clause.**

This keeps the toolkit ethical, safe, and artist‑centered.
