# 🌌 **NDH‑AMS Practitioner Badge v1.1**  
### *Recognition of full practitioner‑level fluency within the NDH cognitive manifold.*

---

## ⭐ **Badge Identity**

**Title:** NDH‑AMS v1.1 Practitioner  
**Role:** Operational user of the NDH manifold  
**Tier:** 1 of 3 (Practitioner → Instructor → Architect)

This badge signifies that the holder can *use* NDH‑AMS fluently in conceptual, analytical, and stabilizing contexts.

---

## ⭐ **Badge Meaning (Plain Language)**  
A Practitioner is someone who:

- understands all nine axes  
- recognizes thresholds  
- maps attachment pairs  
- identifies transitions  
- explains triad geometry  
- walks through loop‑closure  
- analyzes multi‑axis scenarios  
- uses NDH‑AMS to stabilize conceptual complexity  

They do **not** teach NDH‑AMS (that’s Instructor tier).  
They do **not** modify the manifold (that’s Architect tier).  
They *apply* it.

---

## ⭐ **Practitioner Capabilities**

A Practitioner demonstrates:

- **Axis Fluency** — can identify and explain all nine axes  
- **Attachment Understanding** — can map all four attachment pairs  
- **Threshold Recognition** — can identify triggers instantly  
- **Transition Logic** — can map basin transitions  
- **Triad Competency** — understands 06–07–08 stability  
- **Loop‑Closure Clarity** — understands 09 ↔ 01 dynamics  
- **Scenario Analysis** — can analyze multi‑axis cases  
- **Harmonic Integration Awareness** — knows when Axis 05 must intervene
