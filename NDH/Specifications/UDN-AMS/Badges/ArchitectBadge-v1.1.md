# 🌌 **NDH‑AMS Architect Badge v1.1**  
### *Recognition of full manifold‑level mastery and architectural capability.*

---

## ⭐ **Badge Identity**

**Title:** NDH‑AMS v1.1 Architect  
**Role:** Designer, extender, and steward of the NDH manifold  
**Tier:** 3 of 3 (Practitioner → Instructor → Architect)

This badge signifies the ability to **modify**, **extend**, and **architect** NDH‑AMS while maintaining structural integrity.

---

## ⭐ **Badge Meaning (Plain Language)**  
An Architect is someone who:

- understands NDH‑AMS at structural, dynamic, and philosophical levels  
- can design new axes, operators, basins, or attachment maps  
- can refine or extend NDH‑AMS documentation layers  
- can create new case studies and emergent dynamics simulations  
- can evaluate manifold coherence and stability  
- can steward NDH‑AMS evolution responsibly  

They do **not** merely apply or teach the manifold — they **shape** it.

---

## ⭐ **Architect Capabilities**

An Architect demonstrates:

- **Axis Engineering** — can propose new axes or refine existing ones  
- **Attachment Architecture** — can design new attachment pairs or revise geometry  
- **Operator Design** — can define new stability operators and basin structures  
- **Threshold System Expansion** — can create new threshold domains  
- **Triad & Loop‑Closure Innovation** — can extend or reinterpret core geometries  
- **Emergent Dynamics Modeling** — can build new multi‑axis simulations  
- **Documentation Architecture** — can create new NDH‑AMS layers, specs, or maps  
- **Conceptual Safety Stewardship** — ensures NDH‑AMS remains non‑diagnostic, non‑clinical, and non‑political  

---

## ⭐ **Badge Requirements**

To earn the Architect Badge, the learner must complete:

- **Practitioner Badge**  
- **Instructor Badge**  
- **Instructor Guide**  
- **Assessment Suite**  

And demonstrate mastery of:

- manifold‑level reasoning  
- structural modification  
- attachment geometry redesign  
- operator and basin engineering  
- emergent dynamics modeling  
- documentation creation  
- conceptual safety governance
