### 🌌 Governance Drift Corrector — v1.0  
#### *Active re‑stabilization of governance lineage, invariants, and continuity*

---

### 1. Purpose

The **Governance Drift Corrector (GDC)** consumes drift signals from the Governance Drift Detector and applies **targeted corrections** to:

- governance lineage binding  
- governance invariants  
- continuity lock  
- conceptual altitude coherence  
- ethical physics stability  
- governance membrane integrity  

Where the detector says “this is wobbling,” the corrector says “here’s how we fix it—now.”

### 2. Correction model

```yaml
GovernanceDriftCorrector:
  inputs:

    - drift_severity
    - governance_state
    - runtime_state
    - metadata_state
  outputs:
    - correction_actions

```
