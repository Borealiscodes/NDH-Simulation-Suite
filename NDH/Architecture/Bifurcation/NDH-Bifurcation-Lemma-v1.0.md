## 1. Bifurcation lemma v1.0 — statement

Let:

- \(M_{NDH}\) be the NDH native 50‑dimensional manifold.  
- \(M_{anime}\) be any narratively dense external manifold (anime, RPG, Magi‑style world).  
- \(T^{(NDH)}\) be NDH’s native tensor fields over \(M_{NDH}\).  
- \(T^{(anime)}\) be narrative tensor fields over \(M_{anime}\).  
- \(\mathcal{V}\) be the set of visualization‑only mappings \(f : M_{anime} \rightarrow M_{NDH}\).  
- \(\mathcal{E}\) be the set of structural embeddings or isomorphisms \(f : M_{anime} \rightarrow M_{NDH}\).

**Bifurcation Lemma v1.0**

> In NDH, any narratively dense external manifold \(M_{anime}\) may be mapped to the native 50‑dimensional manifold \(M_{NDH}\) only via visualization‑class mappings \(f \in \mathcal{V}\). Structural embeddings or isomorphisms \(f \in \mathcal{E}\) are forbidden. Consequently, no narrative tensor field \(T^{(anime)}\) may be treated as or substituted for any native NDH tensor field \(T^{(NDH)}\), and no point of \(M_{anime}\) may be an element of \(M_{NDH}\).

Formally:

\[
\forall f : M_{anime} \rightarrow M_{NDH}, \quad f \in \mathcal{V} \Rightarrow \text{allowed}, \quad f \in \mathcal{E} \Rightarrow \text{blocked}
\]

\[
\forall x \in M_{anime}, \quad x \notin M_{NDH}
\]

\[
\forall T^{(anime)}, \quad T^{(anime)} \neq T^{(NDH)}
\]

## 2. Proof sketch (architectural, not fully formal)

1. **Dimensional integrity:**  
   NDH’s 50D manifold \(M_{NDH}\) is defined by its own conceptual, ethical, stability, creativity, and meta‑governance dimensions. Allowing \(M_{anime}\) to embed structurally would alter the dimensional basis of \(M_{NDH}\), violating dimensional coherence and Meta Audit Domain 1.

2. **Tensor field integrity:**  
   NDH evolution tensors (e.g. \(R^{(50)}_{ij}\), \(A^{(47)}_{ij}\)) are constructed from native fields \(T^{concept}, T^{ethics}, T^{stability}, T^{creativity}, T^{meta}\). Substituting \(T^{(anime)}\) into these would change the evolution equations, breaking stability and ethical invariants.

3. **Hyperatlas anchoring:**  
   The Hyperatlas maps \(M_{NDH}\) only. If \(M_{anime}\) were allowed to anchor, the atlas would cease to be a faithful representation of NDH’s architecture and become a mixed narrative–structural object, violating its definition.

4. **Governance and ethics:**  
   HRD and GBS v2.0 require that governance and ethical constraints be defined over NDH’s own manifold, not imported narrative manifolds. Structural embedding of \(M_{anime}\) would contaminate governance with external symbolic systems.

Therefore, only visualization‑class mappings are compatible with NDH’s architectural, tensor, atlas, and ethical invariants, and structural embeddings must be forbidden. This yields the bifurcation lemma.
