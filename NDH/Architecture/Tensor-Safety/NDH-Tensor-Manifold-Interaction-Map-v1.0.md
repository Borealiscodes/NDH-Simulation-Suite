### NDH tensor–manifold interaction map v1.0  

---

#### 1. Purpose

The **Tensor–Manifold Interaction Map** describes how NDH’s native tensor fields act on, shape, and constrain the 50‑dimensional manifold \(M_{NDH}\), while remaining within the **Tensor Safety Envelope** and respecting the **Bifurcation Layer** (i.e., excluding external narrative manifolds like anime/RPG worlds from structural influence).

---

#### 2. Core objects

- **NDH native manifold:**  
  \[
  M_{NDH}
  \]
  A 50‑dimensional conceptual manifold.

- **Native tensor fields:**  
  \[
  T^{concept},\ T^{ethics},\ T^{stability},\ T^{creativity},\ T^{meta}
  \]
  Each is a tensor field over \(M_{NDH}\).

- **Evolution tensor (schematic):**  
  \[
  R^{(50)}_{ij} =
  T^{concept}_{ij} +
  T^{ethics}_{ij} +
  T^{stability}_{ij} +
  T^{creativity}_{ij} +
  T^{meta}_{ij}
  \]

- **External narrative manifold (anime/RPG):**  
  \[
  M_{anime}
  \]
  Not structurally embedded in \(M_{NDH}\).

---

#### 3. Interaction roles of each tensor field

**3.1 Concept tensor \(T^{concept}\)**  
- Shapes the **local geometry** of ideas and lenses.  
- Influences geodesics of conceptual paths on \(M_{NDH}\).  
- Determines how “near” or “far” concepts are in the manifold.

**3.2 Ethics tensor \(T^{ethics}\)**  
- Acts as a **constraint field** on allowable trajectories.  
- Prohibits paths that would violate ethical non‑negativity \(e(x) \ge 0\).  
- Modulates curvature to avoid harmful regions.

**3.3 Stability tensor \(T^{stability}\)**  
- Smooths gradients of the stability scalar field \(s(x)\).  
- Ensures \(\|\nabla s(x)\| \le \gamma\) (no sharp shocks).  
- Maintains manifold coherence under evolution.

**3.4 Creativity tensor \(T^{creativity}\)**  
- Introduces controlled variation and branching.  
- Perturbs local geometry within bounded norms \(\|T^{creativity}\| \le \alpha\).  
- Enables generative exploration without destabilizing \(M_{NDH}\).

**3.5 Meta tensor \(T^{meta}\)**  
- Governs **rules of evolution** themselves.  
- Adjusts how other tensors combine into \(R^{(50)}_{ij}\).  
- Encodes meta‑governance and roadmap/audit behavior.

---

#### 4. Combined effect on the manifold

The interaction map can be summarized as:

\[
R^{(50)}_{ij} =
T^{concept}_{ij} +
T^{ethics}_{ij} +
T^{stability}_{ij} +
T^{creativity}_{ij} +
T^{meta}_{ij}
\]

Subject to:

- **Tensor Safety Envelope:**  
  \[
  R^{(50)}_{ij} \in \mathcal{S}
  \]
  (bounded norms, curvature, stability, ethics)

- **Bifurcation Layer:**  
  \[
  T^{(anime)} \notin \{T^{concept}, T^{ethics}, T^{stability}, T^{creativity}, T^{meta}\}
  \]
  and  
  \[
  M_{anime} \not\subset M_{NDH}
  \]

So:

- The **manifold’s geometry** is shaped by \(T^{concept}\).  
- The **allowed paths** are constrained by \(T^{ethics}\).  
- The **smoothness and coherence** are maintained by \(T^{stability}\).  
- The **variation and exploration** are driven by \(T^{creativity}\).  
- The **rules of combination and evolution** are governed by \(T^{meta}\).
