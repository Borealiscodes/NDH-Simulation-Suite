## 2. Audit Criteria

- Tipping surfaces must be defined by a **single geometric condition**, e.g.:  
  \[
  \mathcal{T} = \{ x \in \mathcal{C} : \mathcal{K}(x) = 0 \}

- No tipping surface may be defined by arbitrary thresholds or parameter choices.  
- Tipping surfaces must be hypersurfaces (codimension‑1).

### 2.2 **Codimension‑1 Verification**  
- Confirm that \( \mathcal{T} \) is defined by **one** constraint.  
- Verify that local charts show a hypersurface, not a fuzzy region or multi‑constraint set.  
- Ensure dimensionality is consistent across Geometry v5.0 and Stability Geometry v5.0.

### 2.3 **Curvature Alignment**  
- Basin interiors must satisfy:  
  \[
  \mathcal{K}(x) > 0

- Tipping surfaces must satisfy:  
  \[
  \mathcal{K}(x) = 0
  \]  
- Unstable regions must satisfy:  
  \[

- Curvature gradients across \( \mathcal{T} \) must be bounded and smooth.

---

### 2.4 **Boundary Coherence with Basins**  
- Basin boundaries must coincide with tipping surfaces unless explicitly modeled otherwise.  
- No basin may have ambiguous or multi‑layer boundaries.  
- Basin drift must approach tipping surfaces continuously.

### 2.5 **Operator Compatibility**
