# Chill Dispatch Center

The Chill Dispatch Center (CDC) is the operational middle layer between CDL 
regulatory clarity and NDH conceptual architecture. It provides routing, cooling, 
and vibe-stabilization functions across the tri-architecture ecosystem.

CDC ensures that conceptual load, regulatory load, and nonsense load remain 
balanced. It acts as the dispatch hub for cognitive flow, distributing tasks, 
cooling pressure, and maintaining structural coherence.

The Center is intentionally lightweight, modular, and vibe-driven.

---

## Purpose

The Chill Dispatch Center exists to:

- Regulate cognitive load between NDH and CDL
- Provide cooling and decompression pathways
- Route conceptual, regulatory, and nonsense traffic
- Maintain stability across multi-layer architectures
- Support structured nonsense via the BS subsystem
- Prevent conceptual overextension and regulatory rigidity

CDC is not a lineage.  
CDC is not a conceptual engine.  
CDC is a **dispatch hub**.
